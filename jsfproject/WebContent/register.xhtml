The 2014 FIFA World Cup qualification was a series of tournaments organised by the six FIFA confederations. The 2014 World Cup will feature 32 teams, with one place reserved for the host nation, Brazil. The remaining 31 places were determined by a qualification process, in which the other 207 teams, from the six FIFA confederations, competed. Most of the successful teams were determined within these confederations, with a limited number of inter-confederation play-offs occurring at the end of the process.
